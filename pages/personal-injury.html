The Personal Injury Process

Understanding how we handle personal injury cases from investigation to resolution.

1

Free Case Evaluation

We provide a no-obligation review of your case to determine if you have a valid claim and deserve compensation.

2

Evidence Collection

Comprehensive gathering of police reports, medical records, witness statements, and accident reconstruction.

3

Demand Letter & Negotiation

We prepare and send formal demand letters and negotiate with insurance companies for fair settlement offers.

4

Litigation If Necessary

If settlement cannot be reached, we aggressively litigate your case in court with experienced trial attorneys.

5

Settlement or Verdict

We secure maximum compensation either through favorable settlement or jury verdict in your favor.
